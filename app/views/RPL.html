RPL prototypes

About this work

From 1st August 2022 it will be mandatory to record details of prior learning.

Given the short timescales and delivery windows we chose to add additional pages to the add apprentice journey in approvals rather than to integrate the new data point/questions into the current journey.

* since development payments teams are reworking the add apprentice journey and this may become part of that journey
