Help and support

How to get help if you have questions or concerns during your apprenticeship.

Speak to your day-to-day contact at your employer

You should have a day-to-day contact or manager who can answer any questions or concerns you may have. You can contact them by:

  • arrange a face-to-face meeting
  • phone
  • email

Speak to your training provider

If you can't resolve your questions or concerns with your employer, you should speak to your main contact at your training provider.

Speak to them next time you do your off-the-job training or by:

  • phone
  • email

Contact the Department for Education (DfE)

If you haven't been able to get the help you need from your training provider or employer, or if you need to make a complaint, you can contact us at the Department for Education (DfE).

Telephone

If you have a unique reference number, have it with you when you call.

Available 8am to 8pm Monday to Friday. You can:

  • call us on 08000 150 600
  • text us on 07588 690018

You cannot send us photos or videos via text.

Your phone company may charge you separately, standard SMS rates apply.

Find out about call charges

Email

Send an email to helpdesk@manage-apprenticeships.service.gov.uk.

During our opening times, we'll reply within 4 hours.
