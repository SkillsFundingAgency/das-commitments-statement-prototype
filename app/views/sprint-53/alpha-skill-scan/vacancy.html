What will the apprentice be doing?

Your duties and responsibilities in this role will consist of:

  • Be part of the process around developing and publishing content on our website and social accounts, which will include the various activities we undertake to bring new content to the attention of our audience and start to build a conversation around it
  • Help to develop valuable content that will generate conversion.
  • Support the process to identify potential leads and cultivate our relationship with them
  • Implement systems to capture contact details from our website into our CRM and map that onto a lifecycle for new leads.
  • Put in place reporting against key metrics so we can see how effective our activities are

What training will the apprentice take and what qualification will the apprentice get at the end?

Estio apprenticeship training programmes are delivered virtually by our fully qualified and industry experienced training team. Using their expert knowledge, we’ve purposefully built our programmes around the real-world use of modern technology, so that the skills we create can be directly applied in the workplace.

Throughout the apprenticeship learners receive coaching, help and guidance from a dedicated team who are there to ensure they get the most from their work experience.

Successful completion of this apprenticeship gives you an accredited Level 3 Digital Marketer, with training in how to:

  • Source, plan, develop and deploy digital content across a variety of channels including your website and social media
  • Execute multi-channel digital campaigns using online platforms.
  • Monitor and evaluate channel and campaign performance using different analytical tools
  • Adopt a test and learn approach to digital marketing activities, making recommendations for improvement

What is the expected career progression after this apprenticeship?

The role offers long term security and the opportunity to progress into a permanent position.
