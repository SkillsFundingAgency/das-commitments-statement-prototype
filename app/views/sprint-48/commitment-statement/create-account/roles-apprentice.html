Your responsibilities as an apprentice Step 1 of 3

These roles and responsibilities set out what is expected of you.

I have:

  • read and signed my apprenticeship agreement
  • discussed my training plan with my current employer and my training provider
  • told my current employer and my training provider about any learning needs or health issues I have
  • been told when my apprenticeship training starts and ends
  • understood that this is a portable flexi-job apprenticeship and that I will move between different jobs during my apprenticeship
  • understood the roles of my employers and training provider during my apprenticeship
  • understood that I need to complete the off-the-job training hours stated in my training plan during my normal working hours
  • understood that I will be assessed by an end-point assessor at the end of my apprenticeship training

I will:

  • take part in regular progress reviews with my employer and training provider every 12 weeks
  • attend all my off-the-job training and provide evidence of my recorded hours where needed
  • complete all of my learning activities, assignments and exams
  • share my learning and progress to date with each new employer
  • find my next job when the current job ends, until I am ready to complete my end-point assessment
  • tell my employer in advance when I cannot attend work
  • tell my training provider in advance when I cannot attend my off-the-job training
  • agree with my employer and training provider when I am ready to complete my end-point assessment
  • raise any concerns or complaints through my employer and training providers
