Your training provider's responsibilities Step 3 of 3

The roles and responsibilities set out what is expected of your training provider.

They have:

  • reviewed and kept a copy of the apprenticeship agreement, signed by you and your employer
  • discussed with you how your portable flexi-job apprenticeship will be delivered
  • discussed your training plan with you and your employer
  • ensured that your training plan accounts for prior learning, English and maths needs, and additional learning support
  • told you and your employer your apprenticeship training start and end dates
  • explained how off-the-job training will be delivered
  • told you and your employer if they will use sub-contracted training providers to deliver any of your training
  • explained that you will be assessed by an end-point assessment organisation

They will:

  • manage the quality and delivery of your off-the-job training, and make sure it is recorded
  • support you in finding jobs as you progress through your apprenticeship
  • take responsibility for regular progress reviews between all parties every 12 weeks
  • make sure that you and each employer can see a summary of your learning and progress to date
  • let you and your employer know how to raise concerns or complaints about your training
  • maintain contact with you if you are on an extended break in learning
