Employers, providers and apprentices must be clear on their roles and responsibilities to ensure the success of the apprenticeship.

Provider

I have:

  • a copy of the apprenticeship agreement signed by my apprentice and their employer
  • given the key dates and milestones of the apprenticeship to my apprentice and their employer
  • explained how the apprenticeship will be delivered, including the use of sub-contracted providers where applicable
  • explained how the apprenticeship will be assessed by the end-point-assessment-organisation
  • agreed on and signed a training plan with my apprentice and their employer
  • discussed the training plan with all parties, ensuring it takes account of prior learning, English and maths needs and additional learning support.

I will:-

  • manage the delivery and quality of the apprenticeship, including off-the-job training, as agreed in the training plan
  • be responsible for regular progress reviews between my apprentice, their employer and myself
  • make my apprentice and their employer aware of how they should address concerns or complaints regarding the apprenticeship
  • update or renew the commitment for my apprentice where needed

Employer

I have:

  • a signed apprenticeship agreement between my apprentice and myself
  • worked on a training plan with my apprentice and their training provider
  • signed the training plan together with my apprentice and their training provider
  • I understood and fully support my all my apprentice’s training needs

I will:-

  • will enable my apprentice to complete their off-the-job training during the working hours defined in the apprenticeship agreement
  • take part in regular progress reviews with my apprentice and their training provider
  • support my apprentice and their training provider in addressing and resolving any concerns or complaints
  • update or renew the commitment for my apprentice where needed

Apprentice

I have:

  • read through the details of my apprenticeship agreement
  • understood how it will be delivered by my employer and training provider
  • understood the key dates and milestones of my apprenticeship
  • understood the roles of my employer, training provider and end-point-assessment organisation
  • understood how my apprenticeship will be assessed by the end-point-assessment-organisation
  • been taken through a training plan for my apprenticeship
  • understood that the training plan will also account for prior learning, English and maths qualifications, and additional learning support
  • been given a copy of the training plan that I have signed together with my employer and training provider
  • understood that at least 20% of my apprenticeship hours will be used for off-the-job training
  • understood that my employer supports my off-the-job training
  • not been asked to pay for my apprenticeship by my employer or training provider

I will:-

  • take part in regular progress reviews with my employer and provider
  • attend all learning and development activities, giving evidence where needed
  • let my employer or training provider know in advance when I cannot attend
  • agree with my employer and training provider when I am ready to complete my assessment
  • raise any concerns or complaints I have through my employer and provider’s official channels
  • take my concern or complaint further if I need to and know how to do this
